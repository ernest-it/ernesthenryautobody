Precision Frame & Structural Repair

Advanced frame straightening technology to restore your vehicle's structural integrity and safety to factory specifications.

Structural Repair Excellence

When a vehicle is involved in a collision, damage often extends beyond what's visible on the surface. The frame and structural components form the foundation of your vehicle's safety and performance. At Ernest Henry Auto Body, we have the specialized equipment and expertise to restore your vehicle's structural integrity to factory specifications.

Unibody and Frame Repair

Modern vehicles use either traditional body-on-frame construction or unibody design where the frame and body are integrated. We have the specialized knowledge and equipment to properly repair both types of construction, ensuring structural integrity regardless of your vehicle's design.

Our Frame Repair Process

1. Comprehensive Inspection: We begin with a complete structural assessment using computerized measuring systems to identify all frame damage, including hidden deformations that might not be immediately visible.

2. Documentation: All measurements and damage are carefully documented and shared with your insurance company, ensuring proper repair authorization and coverage.

3. Precision Straightening: Using hydraulic pulling systems and specialized fixtures, we carefully restore bent or twisted frame rails to their original specifications.

4. Structural Welding: When frame sections require replacement, our certified technicians use manufacturer-approved welding techniques to ensure the structural integrity of all repairs.

5. Final Verification: After repairs are complete, we re-measure the entire structure to verify all dimensions match factory specifications before reassembly.

Safety-Critical Repairs

Frame and structural repairs directly affect your vehicle's ability to protect you in a collision. We never compromise on structural repairs—every weld, measurement, and component replacement follows manufacturer specifications exactly. Your safety depends on proper structural repair, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Advanced Welding Capabilities

Modern vehicles use high-strength steel, aluminum, and other specialized materials that require specific welding techniques. Our technicians are trained and certified in all approved welding methods, including MIG, TIG, and resistance spot welding for aluminum and advanced high-strength steel.

Suspension and Steering Alignment

Frame damage often affects suspension mounting points and steering geometry. After structural repairs, we perform a complete four-wheel alignment to ensure proper handling and tire wear. Any damaged suspension or steering components are replaced with OEM parts to maintain factory ride quality and safety.

Quality Assurance

Every structural repair is backed by our quality guarantee and detailed documentation. We provide before and after measurements showing your vehicle has been restored to factory specifications. This documentation is important for your peace of mind and your vehicle's resale value.

Insurance Approved

We work with all major insurance companies and understand their requirements for structural repair documentation and authorization. Our detailed estimates and measurement reports ensure proper coverage while maintaining the highest repair standards.
