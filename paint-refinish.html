Expert Paint & Refinishing Services

Factory-matched colors, state-of-the-art spray booths, and meticulous attention to detail for a showroom-quality finish.

Professional Automotive Painting

At Ernest Henry Auto Body, we believe a quality paint job is about more than just aesthetics—it's about protecting your vehicle and restoring its value. With over 55 years of experience, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional results every time.

Our Paint & Refinish Process

Every paint job begins with proper surface preparation. We carefully sand, prime, and seal your vehicle to ensure the paint adheres correctly and lasts for years. Our state-of-the-art spray booths provide a dust-free, climate-controlled environment for a flawless finish.

Premium Paint Systems

We use only premium automotive paint systems that are color-matched to factory specifications. Whether your vehicle needs a single panel blended or a complete refinish, our technicians ensure seamless color consistency and a factory-quality shine.

Services Include

  • Complete vehicle repaints
  • Panel refinishing and blending
  • Bumper and trim painting
  • Custom color matching
  • Clear coat restoration
  • Scratch and scuff removal
  • Fade correction and restoration

Clear Coat Technology

Modern automotive finishes rely on a durable clear coat to protect the color beneath. We apply multiple layers of high-quality clear coat and then polish it to a deep, mirror-like shine that looks showroom fresh.

Quality You Can See

Our attention to detail extends beyond the visible surfaces. We properly mask and protect all glass, trim, and mechanical components to ensure only the intended areas are painted. After painting, we carefully reassemble and detail your vehicle so it's ready to drive.

Warranty and Satisfaction

We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty on all paint and refinishing services. If you're not completely satisfied with the finish, we'll make it right—that's our commitment to quality.
